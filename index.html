Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Hampi?

    Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Karnataka, India. It was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is renowned for its stunning architecture, historic temples, and vibrant history.

  • Where is Hampi located?

    Hampi is situated in the southern Indian state of Karnataka, near the city of Hospet.
